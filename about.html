The Evolution of NMDC

1992: The Foundation

Brigadier Dr. Nazir completes his specialist training in Maxillofacial Surgery, marking the beginning of a lifelong commitment to surgical precision and medical discipline within the army dental corps.

2010: Visionary Leadership

After decades of leading regional military medical centers, the vision for a premium, patient-first dental clinic begins to take shape, combining high-end technology with military standards of care.

Present Day: A Global Destination

NMDC Dental & Maxillofacial Clinic opens its doors as a premier destination for complex oral health needs, recognized for its clinical excellence and hospitality-driven patient experience.
